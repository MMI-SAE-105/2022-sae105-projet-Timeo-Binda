Glossary

Find in this page the definitions you are missing !

Phonograph
Acoustic device reproducing recorded sounds.
Post-production
Post-production refers to all the stages that occur after the filming of a film. One of the biggest part being the assembly
Red Monstro
The RED Monstro camera is a professional cinema camera.
Reverse Footage
A Reverse Footage a special effect in which the filmed action is shown upside down (i.e. reversed in time)
Tracking
Motion tracking is a special effects technique that consists of analyzing the movements and trajectories of a video, in order to apply them to a new graphic element that will be integrated into this same video.
